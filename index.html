School Achievements

Inter School Table tennis tournament

In the year 2015 Wagle High School, Mangeshi won the Ponda taluka Under-14 boys school team table tennis tournament organised by Directorate of Sports and Youth Affairs at Sports Complex, Ponda.

PONDA: Wagle High School, Mangeshi won the Ponda taluka Under-14 boys school team table tennis tournament organised by Directorate of Sports and Youth Affairs at Sports Complex, Ponda, recently. In final, Wagle HS outplayed S.S. Samiti’s IVBD High School, Dhavali in straights sets 3-0. The team consisted of Atharv Nadkarni, Shreyash Shanbhag, Aniket Naik, Mohit Aiker and Switzel D’Souza. In the Under-14 girls tournament, the school secured second place at the taluka Level. The team consisted of Shruti Mardolkar, Rutuja Tendulkar, Alida Da Costa, Swejal Prabhugaonkar and Simran Velingkar. Sandeep Pagi, PE teacher, guided both the teams. Headmistress and the management congratulated all the players for their success.

What's New

New Computer Lab

Our new computer lab was inaugrated on 23rd July 2015.The computer lab is very spacious with the area of 49 sq.m. The lab is air conditioned. The computer lab has 10 computer systems. We also have internet connection in our computer lab. We have one functional printer. We have weekly one computer class. Our computer teacher's name is Mrs.Savita Manerikar.

New Administrative Office

Our school office has been recently renovated. Office area is 65 sq.m. Inside the office, facilities like potable water and washroom are available. Office has five computer systems, 2 photocopier and 5 printers. School office is provided with internet facility.

New Sanitary Arrangements

Our school has renovated sanitary section. There are five girls toilet and nineteen boys urinals. For girls sanitary pad dispenser and incinerator facility is available. Water facility has also been made available in sanitary section.

New Sports Facility

School has also upgraded sports facility. School ground has multiple sports facilty. Indoor sport facility area is 504 sq.m. There is store room to keep all sports accessories. Sports accessories available are: Foot Ball, Table Tennis Tables, Table Tennis Bats, Table Tennis Ball, Carrom Board, Chess Board, Shot Put, Skipping Rope, High Jump Pole, Jersey, Base Ball Slugger, Cricket Bats, Net Ball, Side drum Bigul, Hand Ball & Volley Ball, Net Pole
